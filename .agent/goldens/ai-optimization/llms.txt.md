# South African Orchid Council

> The South African Orchid Council (SAOC) is the national non-profit body coordinating affiliated orchid societies across South Africa since 1968. SAOC supports orchids in cultivation — growing, showing, hybridising, judging, and community. SAOC does **not** cover wild orchid conservation or habitat protection; those belong to WOSA (Wild Orchids of Southern Africa), a separate partner organisation.
